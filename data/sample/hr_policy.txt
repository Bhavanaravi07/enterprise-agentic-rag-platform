ACME Corp Employee Handbook — Time Off Policy

Paid Time Off (PTO): Full-time employees accrue 15 PTO days per calendar year,
increasing to 20 days after five years of service. PTO requests must be submitted
at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal and approved by a manager.

Remote Work Policy: Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with
manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require VP sign-off and a signed
remote work agreement.

Expense Reimbursement: Business expenses under $500 may be approved by a direct
manager. Expenses of $500 or more require finance department approval. Submit
receipts within 30 days via the expense portal. Mileage is reimbursed at the
current IRS standard rate.
